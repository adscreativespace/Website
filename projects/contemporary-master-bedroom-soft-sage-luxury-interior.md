This contemporary master bedroom was thoughtfully designed to create a peaceful and luxurious retreat that balances elegance with everyday functionality. The design embraces a soft sage green colour palette, bespoke furniture, premium finishes, and carefully curated materials to establish a calm and inviting atmosphere. Custom wardrobes, an upholstered headboard, decorative feature wallpaper, and a comfortable window seating area work together to maximize both aesthetics and practicality. Layered lighting, clean architectural lines, and refined detailing create a timeless interior that reflects modern living with understated luxury.
